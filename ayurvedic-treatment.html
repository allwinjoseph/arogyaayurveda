Our Specialities

For treating specific disease we make available the service of corresponding Ayurvedic Specialist doctor in the course of treatment. Once the patient is admitted doctor recommends a daily routine very special to the patient. It includes special exercises, Yoga, diet & activities. Daily Checkups will be done before & after therapy on a daily basis for analyzing result of treatments.

This ayurveda treatment programme is intended to relax, rejuvenate and regain energy was your mind and body. This programme includes special ayurveda massage services like Rejuvenation massage, Marma massage, Synchronised Massage and treatments like Sirodhara, Pizhichil, Kizhi, Face pack, medicated steam bath.

Swasa Kasa Chikilsa (For Lungs):
A special full body massage treatment to increase the strength of your lungs. This full body massage package is very beneficial for one having Bronchial Asthma, prolonged cough etc. It will strengthen the lungs and will prevent the attack of such ailments. This includes full Body Massage, Vamanam, Urovasthi, Kashaya Vasthi, Pizhichil, Gnavarakizhi etc. along with internal Ayurvedic medicines.

Rejuvenation Therapy ( Rasayana Chikilsa ):
This treatment includes full body massage with hand and foot by medicated oil and cream. It includes internal rejuvenative medicines and medicated steam bath. This full body massage therapy is for 90 minute to 2 hours per day for 7-14 days. It is good to tone up skin, rejuvenate mind, body and soul.

Body Purification Massage Therapy (Shodhana Chikilsa):
It is a process of purifying the whole body to attain proper balance of Vata, Pitta and Kapha. This will also purify doshas of mind viz. Satwa, Rajas and Thamas. This full body massage is a 15 days package process include full body massage, Snehapanam, Nasyam, Virechanam Sneha Vasthi, Kashaya Vasthi, Pizhichil, Gnavarakizhi, Tharpanam, Sirovasthi, Karnapooranam, Medicated steam bath.

You & Your Spine:
This is a special treatment package for your spine problems. It will strengthen the back muscles and prevent degeneration of bones; it can also prevent nerve compression. This is very good for Spondylosis, post traumatic problems (post accidental) sport injuries etc. This treatment includes Body Massage, Snehapanam, Virechanam, Kati Vasthi, Sneha Vasthi, Kashaya Vasthi, Pizhichil, Gnavarakizhi etc.

Body, Mind & Soul:
An absolute stress reliever with a combination of Yoga, Meditation, Ayurveda and Holistic touches. Through this programme your body and mind attain temperance.

Body Immunization / Longevity Treatment ( Kayakalpa Chikilsa ):
This is the prime treatment in AYURVEDA for reducing ageing process, arresting the degeneration of body cells and to immunize the body. Giving Rasayana (very special AYURVEDIC Medicine) internally is the main part of the programme. This package includes full body Massage, Snehapanam, Vamanam, Virechanam, Nasyam, Sneha Vasthi, Kashaya Vasthi, Pizhichil, Gnavarakizhi, Tharpanam, Sirovasthi, Karnapooranam, Medicated Steam Bath, Yoga and Meditation.
( Duration: 21 to 28 days )
